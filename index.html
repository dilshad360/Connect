About Us

Our Mission

Connect tries to nourish the latent talents and creative abilities of students by providing a platform to learn emerging skill sets and equip them to be the faces of the future. The initiative imbibes leadership quality, eloquence and entrepreneurship skills, encouraging peer-to-peer learning and provides internship programs. By organizing talks, fests, job fairs, hackathons, workshops and webinars, Connect aims at creating a focussed platform to hasten the learning process, thereby creating an incentive for student engagement in the exploration of the world that's rapidly evolving.

Our Vision

We paint a larger picture of the student community to create a network with clusters of their area of interests and thus creating a potent workforce capable of unlocking the vast reservoir of untapped potential. By improving specific learning outcomes, it essentially places people first by empowering these people with technical and creative skills to make sustainable choices and multi-dimensional solutions that the complex new world needs.

TinkerHub EMEA

TinkerHub EMEA is a Campus Community Initiative of TinkerHub Foundation, a non profit organization registered in 2016. TinkerHub aims to reduce the gap between technology and its effective employment in lives of tech enthusiasts. By providing an ideal environment to share knowledge and grab opportunities, enabling students to find desirable internships and exposure, we envision a safe space to learn and thrive. This community of students focuses on nurturing co-creation and catalysing a technology learning culture through peer-to-peer learning. This initiative aims at making use of 21st-century technologies and learning methods to foster a fresh breed of highly skilled young people empowered with technical and social skills.

Creative Club

Creative club is founded on the idea of guiding and inspiring students to build creative careers by instilling a feeling of passion and interest for creative and digital skills.The motive of the creative club is to simulate interest in creative ideas among students and seek out to create an awareness of ingenuity in student's mind. This initiative gives an opportunity for students to develop their interactions among groups, helps enhance their innovative, technical and leadership skills. We provide various resources to improve digital skills and to acquire proficiency in digital art. As part of the creative club we conduct various programs and competitions which result in their creative ideas and career build.
